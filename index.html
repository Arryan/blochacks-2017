About us

RefugeeQuest is a data collection platform for the government and United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) to keep track of their refugees. A staff member of UNHCR can access a refugee's whereabouts, contact information, and other data by logging in using their corresponding biometric registration data. A volunteer can also update the information of refugees and write about their current situation.
